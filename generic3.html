This is our video

OUR EXPERIENCE WITH THE V SHAPED STUDENT WAS DOWNRIGHT TASTY

As our group embarked on our next project, the video there was a lot of consideration put into the creative aspects. We went over all the ways we could incorporate the aspects of the V shaped student in an effective yet, interesting way. After long thought and deliberation the Mega Team decided to orchestrate a somewhat "Tasty" video.

In this Hokie video as we deemed the name, we baked chocolate chip cookies and labeled all the ingredients as components of the V shaped student. We realized that all the ingredients of the V shaped student were necessary to complete the VT shaped student module as well as make delicious chocolate chip cookies.
