Kathleen Ann Scarrone

This website has been created and orchestrated by yours truly, Kathleen Scarrone. I am a Literature and Language major here at Tech so I'm just experiencing digital writing for the first time in this course. If you had asked me to operate Atom during first semester I probably would have cried and moved to Alaska. However, with the help of Professor Evias and my good friend and classmate Kendall Daniels I hope to be able to create a website with links to other materials. To the right is a picture of me as a toddler with my cousin Jamie because it's funny.

What I Do in My Spare Time in the Beautiful Blacksburg

  • Benny Marzano's

    Benny Marzano's is a local pizza shop where I order slices of pizza that are as big as my head. I never order a slice of plain cheese pizza without a side of ranch and garlic sauce. Although, the pizza in Blacksburg can never truly compare to pizza I eat at home in New Jersey it is a better substitue than Papa John's. Mr. Marzano's is an amazing establishment and they should open a Benny's in Turner dining hall or Owens.

  • Delta Gamma Fraternity

    As cliche as this sounds I am actually very involved in my sorority. I go all out for other organizations philanthropies because I truly enjoy the competitive, friendly nature but raising money and awareness for worthy causes like Service for Sight are the main reason I participate. Here is the link to a philanthropy I competed in called Watermelon Bust for Feeding America. The video received a lot of notority for the cause of Feeding America!

  • Hokie Football

    I have people ask me all the time: "What even is a hokie?" and I obviously respond, "I am!" Like many other students here at Tech I am a season ticket holder and live for the energy at the football games here. If you have never been in the North End Zone for Enter Sandman you should consider transferring. In addition, if you have never given your best gobble rendition on a key play please consider moving to Alasa or somewhere far away. But honestly, when I think of Tech I always think of the school spirit surrounding the Hokies which, is one of the reasons I was so drawn to this school in the first place. #NoHokieLeftBehind
